About Us

RecycloFlora is an innovative initiative dedicated to tackling the environmental challenges posed by floral waste in India. This initiative is committed to promoting sustainability and creating positive change in our communities.
At RecycloFlora, our mission is to transform floral waste into valuable eco-friendly products while promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility in our communities.

From Petals To Products

We collect used flowers from temples

We give those to manufacturing companies

We then sell the manufactured products at affordable prices
